Characters

Bunny Boy

Bunny Boy is a hyper‑intelligent, hyper‑chaotic hero with a brain that runs faster than light and a body powered by pure cartoon physics. He wears a simple bunny head — not a mask, just his head — and carries an arsenal of ridiculous, devastating weapons he invents on the fly. Bunny Boy can outthink supercomputers, outfight robots, and out‑meme the entire multiverse. He’s unpredictable, unstoppable, and somehow still adorable.

Bunny Boy — Power Stats Bunny Boy’s stats reflect his mix of genius, chaos, and cartoon‑level strength. Strength — 9/10 Can punch through metal, throw giant objects, and tank hits that should not be survivable. Speed — 10/10 Moves like a blur, dodges lasers, and can cross a room before anyone blinks. Intelligence — 10/10 Hyper‑computation brain; invents weapons mid‑battle; predicts enemy moves instantly. Durability — 8/10 Bounces back from explosions, crashes, and cartoon‑style impacts. Energy Control — 7/10 Can channel chaotic energy into weapons and special attacks. Chaos Factor — 11/10 Completely unpredictable. Even he doesn’t know what he’ll do next.

Robuttnik

Robuttnik is a towering, metal‑plated menace with a processor full of evil schemes and a heart made of cold circuitry. Once a genius inventor, now a rogue machine overlord, he commands swarms of robotic minions and plots to turn every living creature into a perfectly obedient robot. His laugh echoes like a broken speaker, and his eyes glow with glitchy rage. He considers Bunny Boy his greatest threat… and greatest annoyance.

Robuttnik — Power Stats Robuttnik is a heavy, armored villain with raw mechanical power and tactical precision. Strength — 8/10 Hydraulic arms can crush steel and throw vehicles. Speed — 4/10 Slow but unstoppable; heavy footsteps shake the ground. Intelligence — 9/10 Master engineer, strategist, and inventor of dangerous machines. Durability — 10/10 Reinforced armor plating; resistant to heat, electricity, and explosions. Energy Output — 9/10 Fires lasers, shockwaves, and EMP blasts. Control Over Minions — 7/10 Commands Cuboids and other robots… when they actually listen.

Cuboids

The Cuboids are small, box‑shaped robots built by Robuttnik — but they didn’t exactly turn out as planned. Each Cuboid has its own weird personality: one is grumpy, one is confused, one is scared of everything, and one just wants snacks. They roll, hop, and wobble around trying to follow Robuttnik’s orders, but usually end up causing chaos instead. They’re technically villains… but honestly? They’re too goofy to be taken seriously.

Cuboids — Power Stats The Cuboids are tiny, chaotic robot minions with wildly inconsistent abilities. Strength — 3/10 They can lift small objects… sometimes. Speed — 5/10 Roll, hop, or wobble around unpredictably. Intelligence — 2/10 They try their best. It doesn’t help. Durability — 6/10 Surprisingly tough for little boxes; they bounce. Teamwork — 1/10 They get in each other’s way constantly. Chaos Factor — 8/10 They cause more problems for Robuttnik than for Bunny Boy.

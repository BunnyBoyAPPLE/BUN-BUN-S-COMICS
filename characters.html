Characters

Bunny Boy

Bunny Boy is a hyper-intelligent, hyper-chaotic hero powered by pure cartoon physics. He invents weapons mid-battle, moves faster than thought, and somehow stays adorable while causing unbelievable destruction. Unpredictable, unstoppable, and always one step ahead.

  • Strength — 9/10 — Punches through metal and tanks explosions.
  • Speed — 10/10 — Moves like a blur; dodges lasers easily.
  • Intelligence — 10/10 — Hyper-computation brain; instant strategies.
  • Durability — 8/10 — Bounces back from cartoon-level impacts.
  • Energy Control — 7/10 — Channels chaotic energy into attacks.
  • Chaos Factor — 11/10 — Even he doesn’t know what he’ll do next.

Robuttnik

Robuttnik is a towering metal overlord with a processor full of evil plans and a glitchy, booming laugh. Built like a tank and armed with devastating tech, he seeks to convert all life into obedient machines. Bunny Boy is his greatest threat… and greatest annoyance.

  • Strength — 8/10 — Hydraulic arms crush steel with ease.
  • Speed — 4/10 — Slow but unstoppable; every step shakes the ground.
  • Intelligence — 9/10 — Master engineer and tactical mastermind.
  • Durability — 10/10 — Reinforced armor resists heat and explosions.
  • Energy Output — 9/10 — Fires lasers, shockwaves, and EMP blasts.
  • Minion Control — 7/10 — Commands Cuboids… when they listen.

The Cuboids

The Cuboids are Robuttnik’s tiny robot minions, each with a weird personality glitch. They wobble, roll, and hop around trying to follow orders, but usually cause chaos instead. They’re villains… technically. Mostly they’re just confused.

  • Strength — 3/10 — Can lift small objects… sometimes.
  • Speed — 5/10 — Roll or hop unpredictably.
  • Intelligence — 2/10 — They try their best. It doesn’t help.
  • Durability — 6/10 — Surprisingly bouncy and tough.
  • Teamwork — 1/10 — Constantly get in each other’s way.
  • Chaos Factor — 8/10 — Cause more trouble for Robuttnik than Bunny Boy.
